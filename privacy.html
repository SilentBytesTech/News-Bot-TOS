NLRP News Bot - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 8 januari 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how the NLRP News bot collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use the bot on the Netherlands Roleplay server. By using the NLRP News bot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The NLRP News bot collects the following types of information:

  • User Data: User IDs, usernames, and roles within the server.
  • Content Data: Messages, commands, and other content generated through the bot.
  • Server Data: Server IDs, channel IDs, and other relevant server information.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected by the NLRP News bot is used for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the bot's functionalities.
  • To improve and optimize the bot’s performance and user experience.
  • To analyze usage patterns for better service delivery.
  • To ensure compliance with the server rules and the Terms of Service.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The developers of the NLRP News bot may share your information under the following circumstances:

  • With server administrators for moderation and rule enforcement purposes.
  • With third-party services as needed to improve the bot’s functionality.
  • As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or other legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of the bot's developers, users, or others.

5. Data Retention

The NLRP News bot retains collected data for as long as necessary to provide the bot's services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact the NLRP News bot development team through the Netherlands Roleplay server.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy. Your continued use of the bot after the changes have been posted will constitute your acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the NLRP News bot development team through the Netherlands Roleplay server.

Thank you for using the NLRP News bot and for trusting us with your information!
